FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why is this book so short?

We worked extra hard to make this book as short as possible. Does that mean we skimp on some details? Sure, but it's done on purpose to make sure that you spend less time reading and analyzing so that you can have more time enjoying your vacation.

It's too short! I want more!

No worries, if you want to dive in further after reading the book, I've created a special resources page for you. On this frequently updated page, I will give you the links to all of the premier blogs and forum threads. This will be your portal to the travel hacking world.

How much work will this take?

Applying for credit cards rarely take more than 10 minutes. Activating them usually take less than 5 minutes. As for reading this book, you should be able to read it all in one sitting.

Isn't this book kind of expensive?

Let's put things into perspective. For the price of a dinner out, you'll be able to travel the world while saving thousands of dollars. The value you'll find in this book is worth way more than the asking price.

Will this information be out-of-date?

Travel hacking evolves very rapidly, but with this book, you will get special access to a frequently updated resources page. What's more, the book itself will get updated from time to time, and you'll have lifetime access to the most updated version!

Is this illegal?

Certainly not. We are simply taking advantage of the offers that credit card companies put out. It's the travel equivalent of taking free samples at CostCo.
