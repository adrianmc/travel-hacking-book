FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why is this book so short?

Why read 200 pages when 50 pages will do the job? I've worked extra hard to make this book as short as possible. Does that mean we skimp on some details? Sure, but we want you to spend less time reading and more time enjoying your vacation instead.

It's too short! I want more!

No worries. If you want to dive in further after reading the book, I've created a frequently updated resources page just for you. Here, I will give you links to all of the premier blogs and forum threads. This will be your portal to the travel hacking world.

How much work will this take?

Applying for credit cards rarely take more than 10 minutes. Activating them usually take less than 5 minutes. As for reading this book, you should be able to read it all in one sitting.

This book is expensive!

Let's put things into perspective. For the price of two dinners, you'll be able to travel the world while saving thousands of dollars. The money you'll save is worth way more than the asking price.

Will this information be out-of-date?

Travel hacking evolves very rapidly, but with this book, you'll get special access to a frequently updated resources page. What's more, the book itself will get updated from time to time, and you'll have lifetime access to the most updated version!

How is this allowed?

We are simply taking advantage of the offers that credit card companies put out. It's the travel equivalent of taking free samples at CostCo.
