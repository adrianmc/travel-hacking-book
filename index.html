FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Why is this book so short?

We've worked extra hard to make this book as short as possible. Does that mean we skimp on some details? Sure, but it's all done on purpose to make sure that you spend less time reading and more time enjoying your vacation.

Will this information be out-of-date?

Travel hacking evolves very rapidly, but with this book, you will also get special access to a frequently updated resources page. What's more, the book itself will get updated from time to time, and you'll have lifetime access to the most updated version!

What if I have more questions?

Send them over to me at adrian@hacktravel.ca and I will personally answer you.

How much time will this take?

Applying for credit cards rarely take more than 10 minutes. Activating them usually take less than 5 minutes. As for reading this book, you should be able to read it all in one day.

Is this illegal?

Certainly not. We are simply taking advantage of the offers that credit card companies put out. It's the travel equivalent of taking free samples at CostCo.
